Physiology Notes

1. Cardiovascular System

Heart

  • Structure: 4 chambers – RA, LA, RV, LV
  • Functions: Pumping blood, maintaining pressure

Cardiac Cycle

  • Systole: Contraction phase
  • Diastole: Relaxation phase

2. Respiratory System

Mechanics of Breathing

  • Inspiration: Diaphragm contracts → thoracic volume ↑ → air inflow
  • Expiration: Diaphragm relaxes → thoracic volume ↓ → air outflow

3. Renal System

Kidney Functions

  • Excretion of waste (urea, creatinine)
  • Regulation of fluid & electrolytes

4. Gastrointestinal System

Motility

  • Peristalsis: Wave-like contraction
  • Segmentation: Mixing of chyme

5. Nervous System

Neurons

  • Structure: Cell body, dendrites, axon
  • Function: Transmit impulses

6. Endocrine System

Major Hormones

  • Pituitary: Growth hormone, ACTH
  • Thyroid: T3, T4

7. Reproductive System

Male

  • Testes: Spermatogenesis
  • Hormones: Testosterone

Female

  • Ovaries: Oogenesis
  • Hormones: Estrogen, Progesterone

8. Muscle & Exercise Physiology

  • Types: Skeletal, Cardiac, Smooth
  • Contraction mechanism: Sliding filament theory

9. Blood & Hematology

  • Components: RBC, WBC, Platelets, Plasma
  • Functions: Transport, immunity, clotting
