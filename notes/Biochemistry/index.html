Biochemistry Notes

1. Carbohydrates

  • Monosaccharides: Glucose, Fructose, Galactose
  • Disaccharides: Sucrose, Lactose, Maltose
  • Polysaccharides: Starch, Glycogen, Cellulose
  • Function: Energy source

2. Proteins

  • Amino acids: building blocks of proteins
  • Enzymes: Biological catalysts
  • Functions: Structure, transport, defense, hormones

3. Lipids

  • Types: Triglycerides, Phospholipids, Steroids
  • Functions: Energy storage, membrane structure, hormones

4. Nucleic Acids

  • DNA: Genetic material
  • RNA: Protein synthesis

5. Vitamins & Minerals

  • Vitamin A, D, E, K – Fat soluble
  • Vitamin B, C – Water soluble
  • Minerals: Ca, Na, K, Mg, Fe, Zn – Functions vary

6. Metabolism (Simplified)

  • Glycolysis – Glucose to Pyruvate
  • TCA Cycle – Energy production
  • Electron Transport Chain – ATP synthesis
